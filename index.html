About Us

Welcome to our restaurant!

Our story begins with a passion for exquisite cuisine and a commitment to providing a memorable dining experience. We take pride in sourcing the finest ingredients and crafting each dish with precision and care. Our diverse menu is a reflection of our dedication to culinary excellence.

Come join us on a culinary journey that celebrates flavors, innovation, and the joy of sharing a great meal. Whether you're a food enthusiast or a casual diner, our restaurant is designed to cater to your taste buds and create lasting memories.

The emerge of ZI-Zest Indulgence

Established in 2020, ZEST INDULGENCE was born out of a passion for exquisite cuisine and a desire to provide a welcoming space for food enthusiasts. Our founder, Mr.Helson, envisioned a place where culinary artistry meets warm hospitality, and that vision has become the heartbeat of our establishment.

Behind every remarkable dining experience is a passionate team dedicated to making it happen. Our chefs, servers, and staff share a common love for hospitality and a commitment to exceeding your expectations. We invite you to get to know the faces behind the scenes, as each member plays a crucial role in making ZI a special place.

Our Menu

Non-Veg Thali

Indulge in our chef's specialties, from the robust Chicken Curry simmered to perfection with aromatic spices to the succulent Mutton Rogan Josh that melts in your mouth. Accompanying the main course are sides like the zesty mint chutney, cooling raita, and a crisp salad that add a refreshing touch to the feast.

Paneer Makhni

We take pride in our signature dish the exquisite Paneer Makhani. This iconic North Indian delight features succulent cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) bathed in a luscious, creamy tomato-based gravy, enriched with aromatic spices and a hint of sweetness.

South-Indian Thali

Experience the grandeur of our South Indian thali, a feast that brings together an array of dishes such as Coconut Rice, Lemon Rice, Poriyal (vegetable stir-fry), and a medley of chutneys. The thali is a celebration of balance and harmony, offering a bit of everything South Indian cuisine has to offer.

Schezwann Rice

Our space combines modern aesthetics with a touch of the Orient, creating an atmosphere that mirrors the fusion of flavors in our dishes. The ambiance is vibrant, reflecting the energy and excitement that come with the bold and spicy notes of Schezwan cuisine.

Dal Makhani

Our Dal Makhni is a labor of love, a slow-cooked masterpiece that embodies the essence of North Indian cuisine. Black lentils and kidney beans are simmered for hours in a velvety tomato-based gravy, enriched with cream and a medley of aromatic spices. The result is a dish that boasts a creamy texture and a symphony of flavors that dance on your palate.

Momos

From the classic steamed momos with a succulent filling to the crispy fried variants that provide a delightful crunch, our menu caters to every palate. Choose from an array of fillings, including chicken, lamb, vegetables, and more.

French-Fries

Elevate your fry experience with our gourmet toppings. From the classic truffle-infused parmesan fries to the bold and savory chili cheese fries, our menu is a symphony of flavors that caters to every palate.

Fish Fry

Indulge in a variety of fish options that cater to every taste preference. From flaky white fish to richer salmon or the robust flavor of catfish, our menu offers a range of choices that promise a unique and satisfying fish fry experience.
